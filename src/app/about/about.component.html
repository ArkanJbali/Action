Full Stack Java Course in Tsofen צופן تسوفين,
As part of the "Employment for High-Tech" program,
we are the 38th course of Tsofen on the subject of
"Full Stack Java" - in Givat Haviva -, with the
participation of 20 graduates and students from
various colleges and universities in Israel.
The course is conducted in cooperation with Tsofen and Amdocs.


- Our Mission -

• Project, in collaboration with Amdocs:
Monitoring and control system that identifies defects
and their severity on Amdocs systems logs for their
technical support team internal usage.
